Cajun Chicken Alfredo Pasta

A beautiful pasta dish marinated in a alfredo sauce of your choice (Homemade is our preference), seasoned with some Cajun Spice Dry rub, and topped with sliced cajun chicken breast.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Alfredo Sauce of yourt choice
  • Cajun spice dry rub
  • 3 lbs Chicken breasts
  • 6 oz Broccoli (optional)
  • 6 oz mushrooms

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Cook chicken
  3. cook pasta
  4. Pour veggies in mix and season well
